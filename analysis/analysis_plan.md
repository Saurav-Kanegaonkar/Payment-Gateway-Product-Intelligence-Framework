# Analysis Plan

## Business Question

Which payment gateway product opportunities should be prioritized when product leaders need to grow approved volume, improve reporting trust, support reconciliation, and prepare experiments for roadmap decisions?

## Inputs

- Transaction daily metrics by partner, channel, payment method, and processor.
- Incident records for data quality and operational reliability.
- Weekly reconciliation records comparing gateway settlement and ledger booking.
- Product experiment candidates with expected lift and readiness.

## Methods

1. Calculate weighted KPI performance across transaction count, gross volume, net revenue, authorization rate, capture rate, return rate, settlement delay, and data quality.
2. Score product opportunities from authorization gap, revenue upside, reconciliation variance, settlement delay, and quality penalty.
3. Score transaction integrity risks from incident count, high severity count, and modeled volume at risk.
4. Estimate six month forecast from recent run rate and early period comparison.
5. Rank experiments by eligible volume, expected lift, confidence, and readiness.

## Outputs

- Executive product cockpit for monthly business review.
- Transaction integrity monitor for incident and reconciliation follow-up.
- Experimentation and forecasting lab for quarterly OKR inspection and roadmap planning.
